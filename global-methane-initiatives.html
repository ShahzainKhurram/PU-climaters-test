Global Methane Initiatives

Efforts to curb methane emissions globally are being ramped up through innovative projects aimed at reducing waste and improving agricultural practices.

A consortium of philanthropic organizations has pledged over $300 million to support the Global Methane Hub, focusing on reducing methane emissions in low- and middle-income countries.

Reducing methane emissions is crucial as methane is over 25 times more potent than CO2 in terms of heat trapping over a 100-year period.

Strategies include capturing methane from landfills and livestock and using it for energy generation, creating a win-win situation for both climate and energy needs.

Global partnerships and local engagement are essential for the success of these initiatives, providing both technical expertise and funding.
